About us


Hello there, fellow plant-admirerer,

Let us introduce ourselves to you.

We are an english garden centre. Our home town in Canterbury, which is an incredibly nice and quiet little place.

Our mission is to help our customers find the perfect plants to their gardens, so it can be the most relaxing place on earth.

Also, we know how much there is to know about how to properly plant and "feed" our "babies", so we thought: What if we were not just selling them to our lovely costumers, but help them keep up the beauty and health of their garden?

That's how we came up with the Garden Tips idea.

We encourage each and every one of our costumers to tell us their problems on our site // on the phone // in person, by coming into our shop.


Make sure to come by some day!
